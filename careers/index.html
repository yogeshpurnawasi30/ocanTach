    An all-star vetting process.

    Our vetting process has been custom designed, keeping in mind the expectations of hiring managers and the challenges faced with the talents from our experience of working with 7,000+ global clients.

    India, the top choice for global companies.

    Indians have been the top choice for global tech firms for over a decade now. This is due to a myriad of factors like the entrepreneurial abilities of Indians, the diverse culture of the country, the standard of education etc. It is for this very reason that companies from around the world tap into India’s vast talent pool.

    Find the right people remotely.

    The talent crunch has made it hard for employers to find the right people. Hiring locally in such a scenario poses many challenges for recruiters like long time-to-hire, difficulty in attracting the right talent etc. Remote hiring could be just the change needed in your approach to help you find the right talent for the right role.

    Choose the talent as per your requirements.

Perks & Benefits

We believe in the well-being of our people and make conscious efforts to facilitate our work family with a favourable work environment and amenities that enables our people to give their best.

Work from Anywhere

We have opened the doors to freedom and flexibility for our people by being Remote-by-Choice, leading to a better work-life balance.

Medical and Accidental Insurances

We have always provided rock-solid support to our people in the times of crisis. To help them in unforeseen circumstances, we have medical and accidental insurance in place for all of our people since their day of joining.

Learning and Development

To help our people upskill themselves and stay updated with the industry trends, we have in-house learning sessions, external training, webinars, seminars, upliftment meetings and various such initiatives under our roof.

Annual Travel Experiences

To help our people set their footprint across the globe, we provide onsite assignments to help them get exposure. Owing to people's goals, we have always tried to create opportunities for them to travel to client locations across the globe.

Potential Individual Earning Opportunities

We strongly believe in work-life balance and work towards helping our people achieve their passion along with profession. We cater ad-hoc projects to our people as and when we have, so they can pursue their passion along in their free time.

Individual Upliftment Program

We believe in constantly learning and upliftment of our people. For the same, we have upliftment meetings with our people as well as invest in their learning and development.

Open Door Policy

We have an open-door policy for our people where they have the freedom to approach anyone in the organization for discussing their concerns. Our strength lies in our people, and addressing their concerns is our utmost priority.

Parental Leaves

We have maternity leaves for a period of 10 days as well as paternity leaves for 2 days compliant with the government rules. We also provide flexible working hours to help them manage both their newborn as well as their work at the office.

Emergency Loans

To support our people in times of need, we provide emergency loans at a very nominal rate of interest. This frees them from complicated & lengthy processes to avail loans from the financial markets.

Life at Uplers

From people-centric initiatives to rewards & recognition to celebrations, we have it all under one roof!

Uplers is a people-first organization. We believe that victories, whether big or small, should be celebrated. We have multiple initiatives under our roof including UpStar, Shining Star, Appreciation Badges, Reward Points with an aim to extend our love for our people.

We also have an internal magazine Uplers Unplugged where people share their stories, pictures and everything happening at Uplers.

We love celebrating every occasion, such as our Founder's day, all festivals and several other milestones. Every year, we have an annual event where the entire Uplers family comes together and enjoys the time together!

Training & Career Development

As part of our continued process, we invest in training and development of our existing workforce to upskill them to be more impactful and grow themselves. The training includes soft skills training, technical & functional skills training and leadership development training.

Our people are our biggest strength. We, at Uplers, are fostering a diverse, inclusive, and growth-driven culture where our people have the freedom and flexibility to share their ideas, explore new horizons and grow. Have a look at what our Uplers family has to share about their experience working with us!
